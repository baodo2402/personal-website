Zermatt - Switzerland

Zermatt is a famous Matterhorn peak in Switzerland.
The car-free community, whose cozily chalets line winding alleys, is surrounded by an aura of the Alps.
The area attracts nature lovers and thrill seekers all year round with its excellent skiing and mountaineering.
